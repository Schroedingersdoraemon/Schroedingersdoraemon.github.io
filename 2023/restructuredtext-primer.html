2.1. math

According to Leonhard Euler, we have Euler's formula which states that for any real number x:

eix = cosx + isinx 
.. math::

    e^ix = cos x + i sin x

Another famous formula inlined here is a2 + b2 = c2.

:math:`a^2 + b^2 = c^2`
